TriVision: VIT-RIT-Industry AI & Data Science
Summit 2025

"Driving Innovation and Collaboration for a Sustainable Future"

January 7-9, 2025

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Chennai and Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT),
New York, in collaboration with industry partners, are organizing the TriVision'25 -AI and Data Science Summit focusing on significant advancements in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. It aims to emphasize the vital role of industry-academia collaborations in driving innovation and impactful applications.
